**PhD Thesis:** *Safeguarding Beyond-5G Wireless Communications with Unmanned Aerial Vehicles: Design and Optimization*
Monash University, Melbourne, Australia, 2022 — *Awarded Exceptional Ph.D. Thesis Evaluation*

## Overview

This research programme developed a comprehensive framework for deploying UAVs to enhance physical-layer security in beyond-5G wireless networks. Across 8 peer-reviewed IEEE publications, the work addressed trajectory design, power allocation, energy harvesting, and machine learning-based optimization.

**Research themes:**
- **Secure UAV Relaying** — Amplify-and-forward (AF) and decode-and-forward (DF) UAV relays with cooperative jamming and SWIPT ([IEEE Access 2019](https://doi.org/10.1109/ACCESS.2019.2948836))
- **PHY-Security with WEH** — Robust trajectory design and resource allocation for UAV transmissions with wireless energy harvesting ([IEEE TVT 2020](https://doi.org/10.1109/TVT.2020.3006216))
- **Reinforcement Learning for Secure MIMO-UAV** — Model-free RL approach against active eavesdroppers in full-duplex MIMO UAV relaying ([IEEE Access 2020](https://doi.org/10.1109/ACCESS.2020.3047662))
- **Terahertz UAV Security** — Secrecy energy efficiency maximization for untrusted THz UAV relaying ([IEEE TVT 2022](https://doi.org/10.1109/TVT.2022.3152904))
- **Aerial IRS for THz Covert Communications** — IRS-mounted UAV enabling covert communication in beyond-5G IoT ([IEEE IoTJ 2022](https://doi.org/10.1109/JIOT.2022.3163604))

**Technologies:** Python · MATLAB · Convex Optimization (CVX) · Reinforcement Learning · UAV Systems · THz Communications · IRS/RIS
