**Postdoctoral Research at:** The Australian National University (ANU), 2023–2025
**ARC Project:** DP220101318 — Physical-Layer Security for Machine-Type Communications

## Overview

This research programme delivered the first systematic analytical framework for physical-layer security under the **finite blocklength (FBL)** regime — the operating mode of 6G machine-type and IoT communications where packets are short and latency is strict.

**Key publications:**
