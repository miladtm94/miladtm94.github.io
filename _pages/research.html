Research Vision & Interests

My research focuses on building the physical-layer intelligence that 6G networks will require — where sensing, communication, and security are no longer designed in isolation but as a unified, adaptive system. I develop analytical frameworks grounded in information theory and estimation theory, then use convex optimization and deep reinforcement learning to make those frameworks tractable under real-world constraints such as finite blocklength operation, hardware imperfections, and adversarial interference. Specific themes include integrated sensing & communications (ISAC), physical-layer security in 6G, ultra-reliable low-latency communications (URLLC), and AI-driven resource management for terrestrial and non-terrestrial networks.
