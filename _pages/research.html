Research Vision & Interests

My research sits at the intersection of wireless communications theory, physical-layer security, and machine intelligence. I develop mathematically grounded and computationally tractable frameworks that make next-generation wireless systems — spanning 5G-and-beyond, non-terrestrial networks, and integrated sensing & communication platforms — simultaneously secure, spectrally efficient, and low-latency. I approach problems by constructing rigorous analytical models first, then using optimization and learning-based methods to scale solutions to real-world constraints.

Physical-Layer Security & Privacy 5G-and-Beyond Wireless Networks Non-Terrestrial Networks (UAV / Satellite) Integrated Sensing & Communications Machine Learning / AI for Communications Statistical Signal Processing Deep Reinforcement Learning Machine-Type Communications
