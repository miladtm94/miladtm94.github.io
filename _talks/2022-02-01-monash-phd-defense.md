Final Ph.D. thesis defense presenting four years of research on UAV-aided physical-layer security for beyond-5G networks. The dissertation covers a unified framework for designing UAV trajectories, allocating communication resources, and leveraging reinforcement learning to safeguard wireless communications against passive and active eavesdroppers. Topics include: secrecy rate maximization via joint trajectory and power optimization, model-free deep reinforcement learning for adaptive UAV relay positioning, THz-band UAV-relay secrecy energy efficiency, covert communications via aerial IRS, and cooperative jamming strategies for untrusted relay networks.

**Supervisors:** Yi Hong, Emanuele Viterbo, Ahmet Şekercioğlu
