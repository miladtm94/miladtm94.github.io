**🏆 Best Paper Award — IEEE ICC 2024, Communication Theory Symposium**
*(top 0.6% of 2,364 submitted contributions)*

Presented new analytical results for the average information leakage of secure finite blocklength transmissions over Rayleigh fading channels. The work provides tight closed-form approximations and reveals fundamental tradeoffs between blocklength, secrecy, and reliability in short-packet communications — directly relevant to 6G machine-type and IoT systems.

**Co-authors:** X. Zhou, N. Yang, A. L. Swindlehurst, H. V. Poor
