Also served as **Session Chair** for the Physical Layer Security Symposium at this conference.

Presented a joint blocklength and 3D trajectory optimization framework for a UAV-enabled relay system under strict latency constraints, targeting secure short-packet IoT communications. The work jointly minimizes the secrecy outage probability subject to finite blocklength decoding constraints and UAV mobility limitations.

**Co-authors:** X. Zhou, N. Yang, A. L. Swindlehurst
